    Neymar

    Football player Neymar da Silva Santos Junior was born on the fifth day of February in the year one thousand nine hundred and ninety-two AD in the Mogi das Cruzes region of the Brazilian city of São Paulo, and lived his early life as a lover of sports, and he played with the Santos Youth Club at the beginning of his career. Neymar became a father when he was only 19 years old, because of the great size in his maturity and development, and he is one of the richest players around the world, and the commercial face of many international brands in the world of football.
