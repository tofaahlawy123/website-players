    Cruijff

    He was born on April 25, 1947, and died on March 24, 2016. He is a Dutch football player and a legend in the Netherlands. He played in the 1974 World Cup final, and he was the runner-up for the world champion, and he also took third place in the European Cup 1976, he played with world famous clubs such as Ajax Amsterdam, the Netherlands. Barcelona and others, and also, after retiring, he coached Ajax from 1985-1988, Barcelona 1988-1996, and the Catalonia football team 2009-2013.
