    Lukaku

    He is a Belgian footballer who plays as a striker for Chelsea in the English Premier League and the Belgian national team. Born in Antwerp, he started his career with Belgian Royal Anderlecht in 2009, making his debut at the age of 16. He won the league's top scorer award. He signed for Chelsea in 2011, but participated sporadically in his first season

    He joined West Bromwich Albion on loan in 2012. He helped them reach eighth place irregularly. Expected in the 2012 season, prior to the 2013 season, he joined Everton on loan. After helping Everton reach Record points tally in club history, join the squad on a permanent deal worth a record £28m fee In 2014.
