    Andrés Iniesta

    He is a Spanish footballer who plays in the midfield with Japan's Vissel Kobe. Andres Iniesta joined Barcelona in 1996 at the age of 12, after he dreamed of playing for Real Madrid, his talents emerged when he was playing with Albacete Youth Club. And after the player spent several years in the La Masia academy, he moved to play in the ranks of Barcelona B club, and that was in the 2000–01 season, and in October 2002, Iniesta fulfilled his dream of participating in the Champions League for the 2002–03 season when coach Louis Van Gal in the match that ended with Barcelona's victory over Belgian club Brugge with an orphan goal. Iniesta was able to find a place for him in the starting line-up for the first team. The beginning of his football career began when he joined the La Masia youth academy, which helped him to join the Spanish under-16 team, and then became an option for the youth coach, which made the first team of Barcelona think about him in order to benefit of it in the future.
