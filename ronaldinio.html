    Ronaldinho

    (born March 21, 1980 in Porto Alegre), better known as Ronaldinho (Brazilian Portuguese: [ʁonawˈdʒĩɲu]) or Ronaldinho Gaucho, is a retired Brazilian footballer. The Brazilian, who is a free kick specialist, has a great ability to exceptional dribbling and quick play. Ronaldinho is known in Portuguese, meaning "Little Ronaldo" in Brazil, by the nickname "Gaucho", in order to distinguish him from Ronaldo, who was actually called "Ronaldinho" in Brazil. Ronaldo simply went by his first name on his move to Europe, allowing Ronaldinho to be abandoned "Gaucho" and only Ronaldinho remains. Before moving to Flamengo Regattas, he played for Milan, and also for Paris Saint-Germain and Barcelona, ​​and won with the Spanish team the Champions League for the first time in 2006 and won the Golden Ball in 2005. He took the award for the best player in the world presented by FIFA annually in 2004 and 2005 and became a citizen Spain in January 2007. Ronaldinho was also chosen to be one of the best Barcelona players in its history.
