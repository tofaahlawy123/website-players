    Maldini

    He is a former Italian professional footballer who played as a left defender and central defender with AC Milan and the Italian national football team. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest defenders of all time, and one of the greatest players of all time. As captain of Milan and Italy for many years he was nicknamed "Il Capitano" ("Captain"). Maldini set the record for the most appearances in the Italian league, with his participation in 647 games, until 2020, when Gianluigi Buffon surpassed him. He is currently the coach of AC Milan, as well as co-owner of Miami FC in the US League - the Championship.
